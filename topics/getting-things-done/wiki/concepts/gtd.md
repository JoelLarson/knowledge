Getting Things Done (GTD) is a workflow methodology for converting ambiguous commitments into trusted external representations and explicit action choices. Its core promise is reduced stress through clarity, review, and reliable execution.

## Core Ideas

- Capture all incoming commitments outside the mind.
- Clarify what each item means before organizing it.
- Distinguish actionable items from reference, someday/maybe, and discard.
- Represent actionable work as projects, next actions, waiting-for items, or calendar commitments.
- Maintain the whole system through regular review.
