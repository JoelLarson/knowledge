A next action is the smallest visible, executable physical action that moves something forward. In GTD, this is the primary unit of doing.

## Characteristics

- concrete
- usually starts with a verb
- executable without hidden planning
- specific enough that you could do it in the right context

## Anti-Patterns

These are weak next actions:

- noun-only items like "taxes" or "insurance"
- vague intentions like "work on proposal"
- disguised projects like "launch website"
