The GTD weekly review is structured enough to think of it as a checklist rather than a vague reflection session. Its job is to restore system trust by clearing inputs, refreshing current commitments, and reopening creative horizons.

## Three-Part Structure

- Get Clear
- Get Current
- Get Creative

## Design Implications

- review output should be structured, not just narrative
- weekly review should have stable sections and deterministic signals
