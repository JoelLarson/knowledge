Waiting-for items are actionable commitments blocked on another person, team, or external dependency. They are not the same as next actions, even if they remain active concerns.

## Detection Signals

- blocked on a named person or organization
- phrasing like "waiting," "hear back," or "pending"
- comments or descriptions that imply an outstanding handoff
