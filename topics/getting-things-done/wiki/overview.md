This wiki contains a GTD knowledge base centered on David Allen's book, a saved Wikipedia article, a GTD workflow map, official review and setup materials, and supplementary podcast and video transcript corpora.

## Main Themes

- GTD is a control loop: capture, clarify, organize, reflect, engage.
- Stress comes from unclear commitments and open loops held in working memory.
- Next actions are concrete, visible actions, not vague topics or disguised projects.
- Review is mandatory for trust; stale systems become noisy and unreliable.
- Official GTD materials treat weekly review as an explicit checklist.
- Delegated work and deferred work need distinct states such as waiting-for and someday/maybe.
- GTD includes a practical setup layer; implementation details materially affect whether the method works in practice.
- The transcript corpora mainly add operational nuance and recurring user-friction patterns; they remain secondary to the canonical texts.
